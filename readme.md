# Xeroq: Smart Printing Queue System for Colleges

## Overview

XeroQ revolutionizes college printing by eliminating queues and paper chaos. Students upload documents online with print preferences and receive a secure OTP. At the xerox center, simply provide your OTP‚Äîthe staff retrieves your file and prints it instantly. No more emailing files or sending documents via WhatsApp. No more waiting in long lines.

## Features

### For Students

- **Upload Anywhere, Anytime**: Submit documents and photos from any device before visiting the xerox center.
- **Comprehensive Print Settings**: Choose copies, paper size, color/B&W, double-sided printing, and photo-specific options.
- **Secure OTP System**: Get a unique OTP that expires in 1 hour for privacy and security.
- **Skip the Queue**: No need to wait in line‚Äîjust walk up and provide your OTP.
- **No Registration Required**: Quick, hassle-free access without creating accounts.

### For Xerox Staff

- **Instant Document Retrieval**: Enter student OTP to access files and print preferences immediately.
- **Digital Queue Management**: See all pending jobs in one organized dashboard.
- **Zero Miscommunication**: All preferences are clearly specified‚Äîno more "How many copies?" questions.
- **One-Click Download**: Download student files directly and print from your system.
- **Mark Jobs Complete**: Track workflow and maintain organized records.

## üìã How It Works

1. **Student Upload**: Student uploads document/photo and sets print preferences from anywhere (hostel, library, etc.)
2. **OTP Generation**: System generates unique 6-digit OTP valid for 1 hour
3. **Visit Xerox Center**: Student goes to xerox center and provides OTP to staff
4. **Staff Retrieves File**: Staff enters OTP in admin panel to view document and preferences
5. **Download & Print**: Staff downloads the file and prints according to specified preferences
6. **Job Completion**: Staff marks job as complete, and the file is automatically deleted for privacy
